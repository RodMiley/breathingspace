Manage Users Debt Respite Scheme (Breathing Space)

Money advisers

Use this service to:

  • Add your client's debts into the Debt Respite Scheme (Breathing Space)
  • Update and add details to existing clients
  • Perform eligibity reviews
  • Check the Breathing Space register to confirm eligibility
  • Request or accept a transfer of a client, to or from, another money advice organisation

Creditors

Use this service to:

  • Review your clients Debt Respite Scheme (Breathing Space)
  • Ask for eligibility reviews
  • Propose new debts to be added to your clients Debt Respite Scheme (Breathing Space)
  • Inform the money adviser you dont own the debt anymore

Before you start

An administrator from your organisation will have given you access via a government gateway account. You will need a govenment gateway account to access this service.

If you don't have access contact your organisations administrator to request it.
